## Naming Conventions Notes

### Name Puns (谐音)
Many names in 红楼梦 contain hidden meanings through homophone puns:
- 甄士隐 = 真事隐 (True events are hidden)
- 贾雨村 = 假语存 (False words remain/exist)
- 贾府 = 假 (False/unreal)
- 甄家 = 真 (True/real)
- 元迎探惜 = 原应叹息 (Originally should sigh)
- 千红一窟 = 千红一哭 (A thousand beauties weep)
- 万艳同杯 = 万艳同悲 (Ten thousand beauties share sorrow)

### Translation Approach
- Keep transliterated names (Pinyin for English, corresponding systems for Russian/Japanese)
- Add explanatory note on first appearance
- For key puns, provide footnote explaining the wordplay
